Summer Concerts

  • Tim McGraw

    July 2, 2022

    Tim McGraw performed at the Stadium Fire. He has some of my favorite country tunes, and was followed by some amazing fireworks. I took my friend Carly and we had a blast!

  • Dustyn Lynch

    July 4, 2022

    This concert was next level. I drove down to St. George with two of my friends in the morning to catch the concert. What a SHOW!!

  • Sam Hunt

    July 29, 2022

    Sam Hunt has definitely been on my country concert bucket list. I saw him in Wyoming during Cheyenne's Frontier Days. He knows how to party!!

  • Brooks and Dunn

    July 30, 2022

    Brooks and Dunn performed in front of a sold out rodeo stadium. The vibes were immaculate as they sang their song "Neon Moon". It was also fun to get my Boot, Scoot, and Boogie on!
